================================================================================
CLAIMS WORKFLOW (Step-by-Step)
================================================================================

BOOKING
└─ Patient provides insurance info (provider + policy number)

VERIFICATION (Before Appointment)
└─ Staff checks eligibility:
   • NHIS: Manual call (0800-900-900) or portal
   • Private: Portal or SMS
   • Status: Eligible / Ineligible / Needs Update

SERVICE
└─ Appointment completed → Invoice created

CLAIM CREATION
└─ Staff clicks "Add Claim" on invoice
   • Pre-fills: Provider, Policy #, Coverage %, Claim Amount
   • Optional: PAR number, diagnosis code, service code
   • Calculates: Patient liability = Total - Insurance

CLAIM SUBMISSION
└─ Two options:
   A) Nexus generates PDF → Staff emails to insurer
   B) Staff uses insurer portal directly (links in Nexus)

APPROVAL & PAYMENT
└─ Timeline: 3-10 days (varies by insurer)
   • VDRL/Hygeia: 3-5 days
   • AXA: 5-7 days
   • NHIS: 5-10 days

PAYMENT TO CLINIC
└─ Insurer transfers to clinic bank account
   • Clinic records payment
   • Patient pays remaining balance (out-of-pocket)

MVP SCOPE (3 WEEKS)

   - UI: Simple form in patient edit modal

2. Claims tracking & management

5. Dashboard summary
   - Claims by status (pie chart)
   - Claims by insurer (bar chart)
   - Total claimed, approved, pending
   - Export to CSV

DATABASE CHANGES:
Patient model: +5 fields (coverage %, annual limit, verified date, verified by, notes)
Invoice model: +6 fields (eligibility checked, PAR #, PAR amount, patient liability, diagnosis, service code)
All backward compatible. One migration.

EFFORT: ~18-29 person-hours (13-19 engineering + 5-10 QA)
TIMELINE: 3 weeks
LAUNCH: Early July 2026

================================================================================
DATA COLLECTION REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

AT PATIENT BOOKING:
1. Insurance Provider (dropdown: NHIS, VDRL, AXA, Hygeia, Glico, ALICO, Other)
2. Policy/Card Number (11-20 characters)
3. Coverage % (50%, 60%, 70%, 80%, 100%)
4. Annual Limit - optional (GHS amount)
5. Secondary insurance? - optional

BEFORE APPOINTMENT:
1. Eligibility verified? (Yes/No)
2. Verification method (Manual/Phone/SMS)
3. Eligible? (Yes/No/Needs Update)
4. Coverage % (confirm or update)
5. Notes (any exclusions or special conditions)

WHEN CREATING CLAIM:
1. Insurance Provider (pre-fill from patient)
2. Policy Number (pre-fill from patient)
3. Claimed Amount (auto-calc as invoice × coverage%, editable)
4. PAR Reference (optional, for major procedures >GHS 500)
5. PAR Approved Amount (optional)
6. Diagnosis Code ICD-10 (optional but recommended)
7. Service Code / Procedure Code (optional)
8. Patient Liability (auto-calculated: invoice - approved)
9. Notes (claim status, submission details)

================================================================================
RISKS & MITIGATION
================================================================================

NHIS Approval Delays
├─ Risk: Clinic can't bill NHIS for 3-5 weeks
└─ Mitigation: Manual email backup works fine; no blocking issue

Insurer Denies Claim
├─ Risk: Clinic loses revenue on that claim
└─ Mitigation: Pre-auth (PAR) before major procedures (>GHS 500)

Patient Data Mismatch
├─ Risk: Claim rejected if name/DOB doesn't match card
└─ Mitigation: Verify patient info against card at booking

Manual Process Slow
├─ Risk: Staff burden managing claims
└─ Mitigation: Phase 2 adds automation when APIs available

Dual Insurance Complexity
├─ Risk: Confusion on primary vs. secondary coverage
└─ Mitigation: Build "secondary insurance" field; show calculation
